About Us

Quiz Time is a simple and interactive platform designed for students preparing for competitive exams like CMAT and Loksewa. We believe learning should be quick, fun, and practical.

With both English and Nepali language support, users can select their quiz set, practice questions, view answers instantly, and track their progress. Whether you're on a desktop or mobile, Quiz Time adjusts perfectly for your experience.

We’re constantly working to improve the platform — adding more sets.

If you have any suggestions or feedback, please feel free to reach out through our Contact Us page.
